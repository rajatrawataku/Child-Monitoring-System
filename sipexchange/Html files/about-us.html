OUR STORY: Why We Do What We Do?

Our team of professionals has been monitoring the Blockchain, Cryptocurrency and Regulatory space since early 2016. We have been mining, trading and investing in coins & various platforms. During this journey we have accumulated a good repository of knowledge and made a extensive international network of professionals & enthusiasts.

Blockchain Technology was built to eliminate TRUST issues. The fundamental objective is to make transactions trustless. It's unfortunate that in the name of Blockchain, in the name of Crypto Trading and in the name of ICO's many people have been deceived in the last 2 years. Every week there is a platform which promises guaranteed returns and majority of times huge returns of upto 50% in a month. Few weeks or months later the project is either in trouble, or hacked or just vanishes. This is the TRUTH.

The stories of people who have lost their hard earned money have begged us to build a platform which is not only open source, secure but also extremely transparent. Our model is to deliver consistent profits for our users through our experience, knowledge and trading discipline. We combine the volatility factor, market dynamics and information of Geo-political shifts; overlay this with trading strategies to benefit from the 24X7 functioning crypto currency markets. Our research group is constantly looking for the best coins to invest for short, medium and long term.

Underneath our strategy is the core foundation which is to protect our user digital capital at all times. Our data driven approach helps us keep GREED at bay which in our experience is the key destroyer of crypto capital. We treat the inherent volatility as a feature to trade and gather profit for our users, our diversification strategy helps us accumulate liquid capital to fulfill our commitments for upto 12 months in case of bear market trends.

On community education, we understand that to track 300+ exchanges and 1500 coins is a highly confusing task for even the brightest individuals. The user community registered with us receive real time and daily information on whats happening in the crypto currency and ICO markets.

For transparency, we publish our top 10 percentage holdings of coins in the platform portfolio. This is available in the live trading section of your dashboard. This helps our users to copy the same trades on various exchanges so they can learn and add more profits to their existing returns from our platform.

Our upcoming feature model portfolio (end 2018) will also use various gamification techniques to provide user to trade and learn on demonstration portal. This will increase confidence of users to research, trade and invest in the real exchanges.
